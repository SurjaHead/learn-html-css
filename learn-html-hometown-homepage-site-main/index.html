Top three activities to do in Ottawa

Visit the Parliament Hill

Parliament Hill offers stunning views of the city and a glimpse into Canadian history.

Walk or skate along the Rideau Canal

The Rideau Canal is a picturesque waterway that opens for skating in the Winter.

Attend the many festivals

Ottawa hosts a variety of festivals throughout the year.
